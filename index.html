Baseline starting points only — Fox 38 Factory / Performance Elite (GRIP2 damper), measured in clicks open from fully closed (clockwise). LSC range 0–18 clicks, LSR range 0–16 clicks, HSC & HSR range 0–2 full turns. Always start from fully closed (turn clockwise until it stops, no force) before counting out. Confirm exact ranges and air pressure chart against your fork's printed spec sheet — internals and model year can shift these slightly. Fine‑tune ±2–3 clicks from this baseline to taste.
